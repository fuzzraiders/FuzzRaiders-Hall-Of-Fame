# 🏆 FuzzRaiders Hall of Fame

The **FuzzRaiders Hall of Fame** is the official recognition system of the team.

This repository highlights members who demonstrate exceptional performance, discipline, and impact across offensive security operations, research, and team contribution.

---

# 🎯 Purpose

- Recognize top-performing members
- Maintain a public record of excellence
- Encourage discipline, consistency, and growth
- Showcase verified performance to external audiences

---

# 🥇 Raider of the Month

Monthly recognition awarded to the most consistent and high-performing member.

This award is based on measurable performance across:

- Work output (labs, projects, research)
- Technical depth
- Write-ups and documentation quality
- Consistency and discipline
- Team contribution (roles, reviews, operations)

---

# 📊 Evaluation Criteria

Members are evaluated based on:

- Work output (labs, projects, research)
- Technical depth
- Write-ups and documentation quality
- Consistency and discipline
- Team contribution (roles, reviews, operations)

---

# 🧠 Philosophy

FuzzRaiders operates on a **performance-driven system**.

Recognition is not based on opinion — it is based on **measurable output, validated work, and consistent execution**.

---

# 📁 Structure

Raider of the Month entries are organized by year and month.

All entries follow a standardized format to ensure consistency and professionalism.

---

# ⚠️ Note

This repository contains only **verified and approved recognitions** by FuzzRaiders leadership.
